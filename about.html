About ME

About me

I don’t like this kind of introduction because it is demanding and boring. I never liked to talk about myself, but if you’re still curious, dear Reader, feel free to read on.

Until I was in my twenties, I didn't even know what JavaScript is, but I wanted to learn something new and exacting. Programming in JavaScript was a big challenge for me.
However, as Socrates said: "All I know is that I know nothing." Anyone who learned programming languages, has experienced this feeling many times. But this profession is like that, because it is always evolving. That's why we have no choice but to learn. There is so much more to learn, e.g. Node.js, Express.js, Python and who knows what all comes next.

That’s why I end my introduction with a famous Star Wars quote: "May the force be with us!"

O mne

Nemám rada takýto spôsob predstavenia sa, lebo je požadovačný a nudný. Nikdy som o sebe nerada hovorila, ale ak si stále zvedavý, milý Čitateľ, neváhaj a čítaj ďalej.

Do svojich dvadsiatich rokov som ani nevedela, čo je JavaScript. Chcela som sa naučiť niečo nové a zároveň náročné. Programovanie v JavaScripte bola skvelá voľba.
Avšak, ako by Sokrates povedal: "Viem, že nič neviem." Každý, kto sa učí programovať, zažil už tento pocit mnohokrát. Táto profesia sa neustále vyvíja, a preto nám nezostáva nič iné, ako učiť sa. Učím sa JavaScript a je toho toľko, čo ma ešte čaká: Node.js, Express.js, Python a ktovie, čo všetko ešte príde.

Tento odsek by som dokončila jedným slávnym citátom zo Star Wars: "Nech nás sila sprevádza!"
